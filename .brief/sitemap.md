## URL Conventions

- All slugs: lowercase, hyphens, no diacritics (transliterate: a->a, a->a, i->i, s->s, t->t)
- Language prefix: /ro and /en (bilingual with next-intl)
- Trailing slashes: no (redirect 301 if present)
- HTTPS obligatoriu (redirect 301 from HTTP)
- non-www (redirect 301 from www.aceagency.ro to aceagency.ro)
- Max 3-4 words per URL segment
